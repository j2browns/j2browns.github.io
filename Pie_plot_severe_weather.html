Average Seasonal Temperature for Summer and Winter in Minnesota

This pie chart shows the relative frequencies of Tornado, Hail, and Thunderstorm events in Minnesota. All events are considered severe following NOAA definitions of severe weather events.

The data set used for this plot extends from 1950 to 1995. In 1996 the definition of severe weather was expanded to include many other events. This reorganization of definitions changed the definition of Tornado, Hail and Thunderstorm/wind events. Therefore the data set is limited from 1950 to 1995 for consistency.

From this chart, over 48% of severe storm events are Thunder/Wind Events,
